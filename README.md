## 👋 About me

I am a **Senior Monitoring & Observability Specialist** and currently the **Trade Watch Platform Solution Architect** at **Grupo Trade Technology**.

My work focuses on designing and evolving observability architectures that help organizations reduce detection and response time, improve operational reliability, and transform technical telemetry into actionable business decisions.

I work across monitoring, APM, distributed tracing, metrics, logs, incident readiness, automation and SRE practices.

> 🇧🇷 Atuo na construção de soluções de observabilidade para ambientes críticos, conectando sinais técnicos, confiabilidade operacional e tomada de decisão executiva.
